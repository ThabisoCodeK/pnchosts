    Mpho Popps is an award-winning South African comedian, TV host, and actor. Popps has made appearances on several TV productions such as Entangled, How to Ruin Christmas and Yoh!Christmas. He is the current host of the South African version of the Masked Singer.

    Tsitsi Chiumya

    Tsitsi Chiumya is an award-winning South African comedian, game developer, and writer. Tsitsi has co-hosted Popcorn and Cheese with both Popps and Robot Boi, and is the script-writer for the South African version of the Masked Singer.

    Farieda Pharoahfi Metsileng

    Farieda Metsileng is a South African content creator, presenter and social media influencer. She played Bettina on UBettina Wethu and made several appearances on Popcorn and Cheese Podcast before joining PNC.
